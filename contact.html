What to include

Helpful context for a faster response

To help us route your message well, please include:

  • A short introduction — who you are and your institutional affiliation (if any)
  • The nature of your interest — research collaboration, partnership, funding, working group, or general inquiry
  • Any relevant domain, region, or method you work in
  • A preferred next step — a brief call, a written exchange, or an introduction to others in the network

We are a network in formation, and your early engagement helps shape what CNCS becomes. Thank you for reaching out.

CNCS works across the many territories and treaty lands that make up what is now called Canada. We recognize the deep knowledge systems of Indigenous peoples and the importance of relational, place-based approaches to understanding complex systems. We are committed to respectful, reciprocal partnerships grounded in Indigenous rights and self-determination.
